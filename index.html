About PLOT Kapital

A Vision for
Africa's Urban Future

PLOT Kapital is a diversified African development company focused on delivering sustainable real estate and infrastructure projects across key African markets. Our strategy is built on combining international development expertise with strong local market knowledge.

Nigeria remains one of Africa's most compelling development markets — with a 223 million+ population, a 22 million unit housing deficit, and a construction sector valued at USD 115 billion.
