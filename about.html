About PLOT Kapital

Building Africa's
Future Cities

PLOT Kapital is a diversified African development company focused on delivering sustainable real estate and infrastructure projects across key African markets. Our strategy is built on combining international development expertise with strong local market knowledge to create projects that generate economic value while improving quality of life.

With projects across Nigeria's most strategic urban corridors, PLOT Kapital is positioned to lead the next phase of African urban development and infrastructure transformation.

Nigeria remains one of Africa's most compelling development markets — with a 223M+ population, a 22 million unit housing deficit, and a construction sector valued at USD 115 billion.

Sustainability

Our ESG Commitment

PLOT Kapital aligns its projects with global sustainability standards and the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals. Every development is designed to deliver long-term environmental and social impact.

We believe that profitable development and responsible development are not in conflict — they are inseparable. Our ESG framework is embedded in every decision, from site selection to handover.

Our projects are designed to generate economic value while improving quality of life — creating employment, empowering youth and women, and building communities that endure.

Advanced water management and recycling systems built into infrastructure design.
